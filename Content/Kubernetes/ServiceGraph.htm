Service Graph

The graphically displays all the connections made between services within and outside the cluster, in a specific time frame. If no connections are made to a service during the time frame selected, it will not appear in the . Every service displayed has an associated .

Services that are outside the currently selected namespace filter are marked with 'Ext' (external).

  • View service details - click on a service to view details about a specific service and zoom in to show its connectivity with other services

  • View vulnerabilities - click on the of a service for expanded details about known vulnerabilities for that service
  • View reputation information for external end-points - click on the end-point

  • Change traffic timeframe

  • Refresh - Click to refresh of the Service Graph.

View Reputation Information

Clicking on an external end-point will display an analysis of the site's reputation including information about the domain and a high-level vulnerability summary.

The analysis information is provided through a 3rd party called Reposify. If Reposify doesn't have data for the site, you will be automatically redirected to the appropriate page on the Talos reputation website (www.talosintelligence.com).

Traffic Time Frame

The Traffic Time Frame is the length of time that considers a connection as active. Connections are displayed in the Service Graph for services that have had active traffic during the time frame selected. If no traffic has originated from or into a service during the time frame selected, that service will not appear in the Service Graph.
